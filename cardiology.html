Advanced Cardiovascular Sciences

Cardiology Department

A premier center for comprehensive cardiac care, providing highly advanced diagnostic tracking, interventional therapies, and rehabilitative management.

Our department operates state-of-the-art flat-panel digital catheterization labs and hybrid operating suites to address complex cardiovascular conditions with extreme accuracy and speed.

Book Cardiac Consultation

Conditions Handled

  • Coronary Artery Disease: Managing ischemic constraints and vascular blockages.
  • Congestive Heart Failure: Advanced fluid dynamics monitoring and treatment frameworks.
  • Arrhythmias & Blocks: Rhythmic monitoring combined with internal electrophysiology targeting.
  • Valvular Heart Diseases: Advanced clinical response structures managing severe structural regurgitations.
  • Congenital Heart Defects: Tailored support pipelines monitoring adult lifecycle adaptations.

Diagnostic Infrastructure

  • 3D Echocardiography: Live spatial mapping assessing real-time mechanical efficiency.
  • Holter Systems: Continuous multi-day electrocardiograph logging profiles.
  • Stress Echo Assemblies: Stress workout performance capturing arterial response limits.
  • 128-Slice CT Scanning: Detailed vascular reconstructions tracking plaque details safely.
  • Electrophysiology Suite: Precision monitoring fields plotting arrhythmia pathways directly.

Clinical Procedures

  • Primary Angioplasty (PTCA): Rapid non-surgical coronary vessel opening for emergency situations.
  • Bypass Grafting (CABG): Micro-surgical artery path adjustments handling critical blockages safely.
  • Pacemaker Interventions: Long-term mechanical heart rhythm management options.
  • TAVI Implantation: Transcatheter aortic valve updates using modern non-invasive deployment setups.
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation: Medical aerobic strength retraining regimens.

Advanced Treatment Technology

Intravascular Ultrasound

Internal cross-sectional echo tracking providing high-accuracy configuration mappings for custom structural stents.

Hybrid Operating Suites

Integrates cutting-edge diagnostic radiology imaging fields with fully structured cardiac surgery modules simultaneously.

Optical Coherence Tomography

Utilizes precise near-infrared light paths to construct highly valuable micron-scale arterial views.
