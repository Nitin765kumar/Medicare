Advanced Interventional Pulmonology

Pulmonology Department

Dedicated center for obstructive lung diseases, specialized interstitial lung conditions, and comprehensive diagnostic sleep disorders management.

Our testing rooms are configured with computerized body plethysmography tools and diagnostic bronchoscopes for detailed deep-tissue structural tracing.

Book Pulmonologist

Pulmonary Function

  • Advanced Spirometry: High-precision diagnostic verification mapping structural lung volumes.
  • Diffusion Capacity: Specialized DLCO tracking parsing gas transfer rate barriers.
  • Arterial Blood Gas: Real-time ABG analytical mapping evaluating system gas balance.
  • Oxygen Profiling: Monitored exertion-induced oxygen tracking protocols.

Interventional Units

  • Flexible Bronchoscopy: Internal fiber-optic lung path visualizations.
  • Needle Aspiration: Transbronchial deep tissue micro-sampling (TBNA arrays).
  • Pleural Drainage: Ultrasound-targeted safety lines draining accumulated cavity fluids.
  • Airway Interventions: Advanced structural foreign body extractions and dilation controls.

Sleep Medicine

  • Polysomnography: Comprehensive clinical diagnostic overnight sleep telemetry.
  • Apnea Profiling: Specialized tracking arrays recording Obstructive Sleep Apnea.
  • CPAP Pressure Titration: Individual dynamic machine configurations maximizing flow safety.
  • Hypersomnia Tracking: Multi-latency wakefulness mapping verifying severe fatigue layers.
